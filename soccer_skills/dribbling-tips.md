# Dribbling Tips

3 Styles of dribbling.

### Nudge

Ball within 1-2 feet.

### Mid Range

keep the ball within 3-5 feet.

### Long Range

10+ Yards



Key point: **Use the top of your foot near year toes to push the ball forward.**
